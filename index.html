Travel to Sunny Susanville!

A wonderful little town nestled where the forest meets the desert

Things to see:

  • The Susan River: You'll be swept away!
  • Susanville Ranch Park: BYOH - Bring your own horse
  • Lassen Historical Museum: Explore the little known history of the area

Places to go:

  • The Native American Casino: A knee slappin' good time
  • The Frosy Mill: Just like Mellancamp wrote in 'Jack and Diane' - "Suckin' on chilli dog outside the Frosty Mill"
  • Wal-Mart: This town has a Wal-Mart!
